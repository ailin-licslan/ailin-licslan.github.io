The metropolis of Sydney is a wonderland of unique experience bursting with beauty.
Nestled between beaches and national parks,Sydneysiders enjoy many natural attractions.
As one of the most livable cities in the world, millions of international tourists travel down under every year.
It's no secret that Sydney is picturesque - anyone can recognize the iconic Sydney Open House.
But its thriving economy and vibrant yet laid-back culture make it perfect for anyone's tastes.
Speaking of tastes, Sydney is known fot its wine, seafood, coffee experts, and avocado on toast!
Although Sydney is synonymous with sun-bathed beaches like Bondi or Manly Beach, it's also home to a rich and sensitive ecology.
Western Sydney Parklands has a unique biodiversity of flora and fauna, which the city is fighting to keep pristine.
Sydney's majestic coastline is full of marine species, and Sydney Harbor National Park is a great place to drink it all in. Just whatever you do, don't forget your sunscreen!
